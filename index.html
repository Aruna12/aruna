Hello, I am Aruna!

I am currently a student at the Melbourne Business School at The University of Melbourne pursuing Masters in Business Analytics.

I have an undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Engineering. During my undergrad, I got the opportunity to participate in a hackathon. This was the first time I used machine learning to develop a web application to predict gold prices. After this experience, I was keen on diving deep into this path and over my coursework I got to work on more projects using machine learning and text analytics.

While I enjoyed developing different models to predict outcomes, I realized I lacked the knowledge and skills to use these insights to solve business problems. Hence, I decided to pursue my masters to bridge the gap between my technical and business skills to be able to help companies make a data-driven decision.

During my masters program, I have gained knowledge in statistical learning, predictive analytics, optimisation and causal inference. I got to fine tune my skills in machine learning and text analytics. I have been able to work with people from diverse academic backgrounds which has not only taught me the importance of team work but also effective communication, which I realized is equally important compared to technical skills.

I have experience in text analytics on topics such as text classification, brand identification, data mining and data crawling. Check out my projects and experiences.

My next step is to work as a data analyst where I can apply my skills on product and customer data to obtain insights and help make better decisions that can improve customer experience.
